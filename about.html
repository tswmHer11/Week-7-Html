How Overland Express got started


Overland Express came to life when a group of friends whose dreams were to turn there everyday vehicles into adventure mobiles. They were experienced engineers who wanted the ability to take their vehicles on weekend adventures and also use them as their daily drivers. With there experience and enginuity, they started creating products for their own vehicles. They liked it so much, they decided to make their own company because they knew other people had their same dreams but didn't know how or where to look.

We are a small company that specializes in creating Overlanding vehicles for our customers. You can purchase one or more of our many products or for an additional fee we can custom fit or make something you desire. Our company consists of experienced professional welders, fabricators and engineers that may assist you in finding whats best for your vehicle.
